2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

Our emotion detection feature uses your camera to analyze facial expressions. This data is processed locally and securely to provide personalized AI responses. Camera access requires your explicit permission and can be disabled at any time.

Your data is stored securely using Firebase Firestore with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can manage your data through your account settings or by contacting us.
